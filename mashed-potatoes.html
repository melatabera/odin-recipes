Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Description

A quick and easy mashed potato recipe with a garlicky flavor

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk and Butter
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Step-by-Step Directions

  1. Combine potatoes, garlic, and salt in a medium saucepan with water
  2. Boil over high, reduce heat to medium low and simmer potatoes until tender
  3. Drain the potatoes and add butter, 1 peice at a time
  4. Using a potator masher, mash in milk until smooth and add seasonings
