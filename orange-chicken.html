Orange Chicken

Description

A sticky, sweet orange chicken recipe that anyone can make!

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • Water
  • Rice vinegar
  • Lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Packed brown sugar
  • Chopped green onion
  • Grated orange zest
  • Minced ginger root
  • Minced Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Cornstarch

Chicken:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Step-by-Step Directions

  1. Combine ingredients for sauce over medium-high heat. Remove when boiled and cool for 10-15 minutes
  2. Place chicken in plastic bag and pour 1 cup of sauce in bag. Seal and refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  3. Remove chicken and coat in flour, salt, and pepper
  4. Heat the olive oil in medium heat and cook chicken until browned on both sides
  5. Remove to paper towed-lined plate and cover with aluminum foil
  6. Pour reserved sauce into the skillet and bring to boil. Mix together cornstartch and water until smooth. Stir into boiling sauce
  7. Reduce heat to medium low, add the chiken, and simmer
  8. Serve over rice and enjoy
